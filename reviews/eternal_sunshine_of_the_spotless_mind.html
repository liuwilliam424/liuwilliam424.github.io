Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

I'm having a good time
January 7, 2024

So I’ve heard of this movie before, which means that I thought it would be good. And you know what? It was good. But also, it’s probably entirely overrated. I just looked at this list. (At the time of writing this, the words 'this list' don't properly show that it's a link, but if you click on those words it should still properly lead to the right site.) And it really doesn’t seem to deserve that number 6 spot. Though, to be fair, I don’t agree with the order of the movies there either.

My takeaway is that you should try to live in the moment and enjoy life. Because you’re going to forget things anyways. And also, the ending had a very Arrival-esque feel to it, where they know the relationship will end in disaster, but are going to pursue it anyways.
